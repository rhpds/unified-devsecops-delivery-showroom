= Conclusion and Next Steps

Congratulations! You've completed the *Unifying DevSecOps: Secure, Standardized Application Delivery Across Containers* workshop.

== What You've Learned

Throughout this workshop, you’ve seen how a unified platform enables secure, standardized application delivery across environments.

* You established software composition trust by ingesting and analyzing SBOMs, gaining clear visibility into components and vulnerabilities.
* You implemented identity-based signing and verification, ensuring every artifact is cryptographically linked to a trusted source.
* You used an Internal Developer Portal to provide developers with a self-service, standardized path to create and deliver applications.
* You enforced policy-driven promotion, ensuring only verified, compliant software is allowed to progress through the delivery pipeline.

*Most importantly, you saw how these capabilities work together as a single, integrated workflow, not as isolated tools.*

---

== Key Takeaways

. *Standardization Enables Scale*: Developers follow a consistent, approved path to production, regardless of whether applications run on containers or virtual machines.

. *Trust Is Continuously Verified*: From code to deployment, identity, integrity, and provenance are validated at every step, not just at release time.

. *Security Without Friction*: Security, compliance, and governance are embedded into the platform, allowing developers to focus on building features rather than managing infrastructure or controls.

. *Policy-Driven Automation Reduces Risk*: Promotion decisions are enforced automatically through policy, ensuring that only trusted and compliant software reaches production.

---

== What This Means for Your Organization

* A consistent and repeatable path to deliver applications across environments
* Reduced risk of untrusted or vulnerable software reaching production
* Full visibility and auditability of the software delivery lifecycle
* Faster delivery cycles without sacrificing security or compliance

*You can move faster, with confidence, because trust and governance are built into the platform by design.*
